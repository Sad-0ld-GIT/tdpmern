What are e-sports?

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a form of competition using video games. Esports often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams.

Most popular games

Here are some of the top games gamers compete in:

Apex Legends
Free-to-play game where players compete in different modes, such as Battle Royale, Arenas, and limited-time events
Call of Duty: Warzone 2.0
Battle royale mode within the Call of Duty Franchise
Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Multiplayer first-person shooter game
